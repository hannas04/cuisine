Our Story

Crafting Culinary Excellence

A Legacy of Flavor

Founded in the heart of Paris, Cuisine Fantastique has been redefining the art of catering for over two decades. Our journey began with a simple vision: to transform every event into an unforgettable culinary experience.

We blend traditional French techniques with innovative international flavors, creating dishes that are not just meals, but memories. Every ingredient is carefully sourced, every dish meticulously crafted, and every event thoughtfully orchestrated.

From intimate gatherings to grand celebrations, we bring the same level of passion, precision, and perfection to every occasion. Our team of world-class chefs and event specialists work tirelessly to exceed expectations and create moments that linger long after the last course.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?

+

We recommend booking at least 3-6 months in advance for large events like weddings and galas. For smaller gatherings and corporate events, 4-6 weeks is typically sufficient. However, we occasionally accommodate last-minute requests based on availability.

Can you accommodate dietary restrictions?

+

Absolutely! We specialize in creating delicious menus for all dietary needs including vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, kosher, halal, and various food allergies. Simply let us know during the consultation, and we'll design a menu that ensures everyone can enjoy the experience.

What is included in your catering packages?

+

Our packages include menu planning, food preparation, professional service staff, elegant table settings, and complete setup and cleanup. We also offer optional add-ons such as specialty beverages, custom cake design, floral arrangements, and event coordination services.

Do you provide staff for the event?

+

Yes! We provide fully trained, professional service staff including servers, bartenders, and event coordinators. The number of staff members is determined based on your guest count and service style to ensure impeccable service throughout your event.

What areas do you serve?

+

We are based in Paris, London, and New York, and primarily serve these metropolitan areas and surrounding regions. For destination events or locations outside our standard service area, please contact us to discuss arrangements. We've successfully catered events across Europe and North America.

Can we schedule a tasting before booking?

+

Yes! We encourage all clients to schedule a tasting session. This allows you to sample our cuisine and ensure it meets your expectations. Tastings are typically scheduled after the initial consultation and menu design phase, and can be customized to reflect your proposed menu.
